About Me

Tanya McNamara is an accomplished healthcare leader and Registered Nurse with more than 20 years of experience spanning emergency nursing, community health, chronic disease management, rehabilitation, digital health, and clinical governance. She holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) with a specialisation in Human Resource Management and combines extensive clinical expertise with a strong background in strategic leadership, operational management, workforce development, and quality improvement.

Throughout her career, Tanya has led multidisciplinary clinical teams across Australia, overseeing the delivery of complex healthcare programs, clinical governance frameworks, service transformation initiatives, and large-scale workforce operations. Her experience includes leadership roles within national healthcare organisations, where she was responsible for program performance, clinical risk management, stakeholder engagement, quality systems, and the implementation of innovative models of care designed to improve client outcomes and service accessibility.

As Head of Clinical Care at Symbol Group, Tanya is committed to fostering a culture of clinical excellence, safety, and continuous improvement. She is passionate about ensuring that every individual receiving care experiences services that are not only clinically robust and evidence-based but also delivered with dignity, compassion, and genuine hospitality. Her leadership approach focuses on building high-performing teams, strengthening clinical governance, and creating exceptional aged care experiences that place the individual at the centre of every decision.

Tanya is dedicated to supporting Symbol Group's vision of delivering outstanding care through strong clinical frameworks, person-centred practices, and a relentless focus on quality, innovation, and positive outcomes for residents, clients, families, and staff.
